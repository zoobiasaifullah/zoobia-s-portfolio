About Me

I'm Zoobia, a third-year HCI student at NJIT's Albert Dorman Honors College with a passion for designing products that solve problems and feel natural to use.

My design process revolves around deep listening, noticing patterns, and genuinely connecting with people. I enjoy figuring out why someone abandons a checkout flow or hesitates before tapping a button, because understanding these moments are key to creating better experiences.

I pay close attention to small details and behaviors that often go unnoticed. I'm naturally reflective, always trying to grow, and driven by the hope that my work, even in small ways, can make someone's day a little better.
